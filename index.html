About Me

A short, honest introduction — not a highlight reel.

Most portfolios open with a confident summary of expertise. Mine won't, because it wouldn't be honest. I'm early. I don't have a long list of finished, client-tested work — what I have is momentum, curiosity, and a habit of actually finishing what I start.

Before this, I spent time exploring different corners of the internet — including some short-lived crypto/airdrop projects that taught me things but weren't a direction I wanted to build a future on. What stuck was building things with AI-assisted development tools — planning a project, breaking it into steps, and actually shipping it.

I'm not interested in performing confidence I don't have yet. I'd rather be specific about where I am, and let the work speak for the trajectory.
