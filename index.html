About

Starting from zero, on purpose.

I'm Abu-Huraira — a 19-year-old undergraduate who didn't want to follow a fixed, predictable plan. Instead, I spent time exploring different corners of the internet, experimenting, and figuring out what actually held my attention.

What stuck was building things with AI-assisted tools. I don't come from a formal computer science background — everything here is self-directed, learned by doing, breaking things, and trying again. This portfolio itself is proof of that process: planned, built, and refined step by step.

I'm not pretending to be an expert. I'm early, honest about it, and moving with real momentum. Right now I'm looking for opportunities — an internship, an entry-level role, or a collaboration — where that momentum can turn into something bigger.
